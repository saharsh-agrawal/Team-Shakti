  • WHO ARE WE?

    Team Shakti is a unique initiative by some students of IIT Kharagpur which focuses on the field of Sustainable Energy Technologies. It is mapped out as a research-based society for students in the college to work together in making technological innovations for a sustainable future.

    Last updated 3 mins ago

    WHAT WE DO?

    We intend to work from the grassroots level and bring changes like achieving the goal of a carbon-negative campus. We aspire to win national and international clean energy competitions and provide clean, green, and efficient solutions to the energy problems of our planet, starting from taking care of our IIT campus energy requirements.
